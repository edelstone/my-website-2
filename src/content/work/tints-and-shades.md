The [Tint & Shade Generator](https://maketintsandshades.com) is an open-source web app for accurately producing tints and shades from a given hex color in configurable increments. It has become one of the most popular color tools on the web, used by designers, developers, educators, and data analysts in over 180 countries.

- [Website](https://maketintsandshades.com)
- [Source code](https://github.com/edelstone/tints-and-shades)
