[Texas State University](https://txst.edu) is a renowned institution that had fallen behind digitally. Although the university converted to a custom CMS in the early 2000s, shifting priorities left front-end templates largely unchanged, and publishers struggled to present information effectively.

I identified and led several initiatives to close this gap, resulting in 1) an accessible, multi-column template used by hundreds of content creators, 2) an admissions-focused, responsive homepage, and 3) a flexible digital identity documented to guide future product updates. Each represented a first for the university.
