[Tock](https://edelstone.github.io/tock/) is a super-minimal menu bar timer for macOS. Designed for speed and focus, it’s keyboard-driven, supports natural-language input, and intentionally centers on a single active timer to keep the interface uncluttered. Keyboard shortcuts, notification tones, repeat behavior, and volume are all configurable.
