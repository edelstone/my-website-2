[GTreasury](https://gtreasury.com) builds treasury management software for governments and large institutions. Its primary application dates back to the early days of the web and had become fragmented and inconsistent over time.

I led a ground-up redesign and content strategy, delivering structured, component-based artifacts designed to endure as teams transitioned to a modern JavaScript framework. The resulting design system, nicknamed _Obelisk_, was immediately used to prototype high-priority user flows and prove its value.

Partnering with a front-end engineer, I wrote the CSS for a [pixel-perfect coded prototype](https://gtreasury.netlify.app) that brought the system to life.
